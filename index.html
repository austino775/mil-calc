What Is Mil-Calc?

Mil-Calc is a fast, digital distance milling calculator.

It replaces traditional slide rules with a clean, readable interface built for ease of use — whether you're at the range, hunting, or practicing fundamentals. No more slide rules and no more more math while out in the field.

Why Milling Still Matters

Milling is a fundamental shooting skill and a reliable backup when electronics or rangefinders fail.

It reinforces ballistic understanding — and honestly, it’s kind of fun.

Mil-Calc keeps the skill intact while removing friction.

How to Use Mil-Calc

  1. Measure the target’s actual size accurately (height or width).
  2. Observe the target through your optic and read the MIL or MOA measurement.
  3. Enter both values into the app.
  4. Instantly receive the calculated distance in yards or meters.

Accuracy matters: milling depends on knowing the target’s real-world size as precisely as possible.
